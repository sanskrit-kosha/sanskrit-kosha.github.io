ārtagala

(1) Barleria strigosa Willd. (Bpn, puṣpavarga 51-52; DGV 5, p. 293-
295; Kai.Nigh., oṣadhivarga 1049: B. strigosa Linn.; KB 3, p. 1880-1881)
= B. caerulea Roxb. (MW; PW; Vśs); cf. DWH 3, p. 44-45: Barleria species;
(2) B. cristata Linn. (Rā.nigh., ed. Tripāṭhī, karavīrādivarga 133:
ārtagalā);
(3) Xanthium strumarium Linn. (Bt.; L. S. Bhatnagar et al. in JRIM 8,
2, 1973, p. 97); see DGV 5, p. 34-35;
(4) Acanthus ilicifolius Linn. (Bt.);
(5) = kakubha (Ḍa. on Su.Sū. 38, 10, Sū. 39, 7, Ci. 7, 6, U. 9, 19 and U. 53,
11; see DGV 5, p. 34-35: probably Lactuca serriola Linn. or L. indica
Linn.);
(6) = kaṇṭakīvṛkṣa (Ḍa. on Su.Sū. 39, 7) or kaṇṭakaphala (Ḍa. on Su.U.
9, 19); see DGV 5, p. 34-35;
(7) = hintāla (Indu on A.s.Ci. 20, 4: according to some authorities).
