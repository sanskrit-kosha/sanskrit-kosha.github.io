amilāta(ka), amlāṭana, amlāta(ka), amlāna(ka), amlāyana(ka)

(1) = saireyaka = jhiṇṭī (Nś. 245-246: amilāta, amlāta);
a) the variety with red flowers is identical with kurabaka or
kurubaka (Nś. 245-246: kurabaka; Rā. nigh. 10, 412-413: raktāmlāna
= kurubaka);
b) the variety with yellow flowers is identical with kuraṇṭaka
or kuraṇḍaka (Nś. 245-246: kuraṇṭaka; Rā.nigh. 10, 414-415:
pītāmlāna = kuraṇṭaka; Indu on A.s.U. 40, 71: kuraṇḍaka = pītām-
lāyanaka);
c) the variety with blue flowers is identical with ārtagala (on
which see below) (Nś. 247ab; Rā.nigh. 10, 416-417: nīlāmlāna =
ārtagala).
Cf. Amarakośa 2, 4, 74-76ab; Bpn, puṣpavarga 49: amlāṭana = kuraṇṭaka;
Kai. Nigh., oṣadhivarga 1047-1050ab: amlāna = jhiṇṭī = sairyaka; Kai.
Nigh., oṣadhivarga 1525-1526: amlāyana = amlātaka = amlānaka: unidenti-
fied; Trikāṇḍaśeṣa 2, 4, 25: amlāna = kuraṇṭaka. See sairīyaka = saireyaka.
(2) = rājataraṇī (Rā.nigh. 10, 246: amilātaka, amlāna): unidentified,
but probably a kind of rose;
(3) Gomphrena globosa Linn. (MW; PW; Vśs: amlāna);
(4) = bandhujīvaka (Vśs): Pentapetes phoenicea Linn.
