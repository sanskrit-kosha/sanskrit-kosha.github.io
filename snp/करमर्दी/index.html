karamardī

Su. Sū. 38, 73 (omitted in Bt.), Ḍa.: karamardī kṣīraphalā ‘karamadī’
iti loke. Śiva. Dr., phalavarga 11 (karamarda): karamardaṃ kaṇṭakīpha-
lam iti candrikā, tad dvividhaṃ mahad alpaṃ ca. tatra mahat prāyaśo
grāmabhavam amlaṃ ca. alpā tu karamardī vanajā madhurāmlaphalā iti ca
vyācaṣṭe. See also Bt. on karamarda: karamarda is known as Carissa
carandas Linn. and its wild kind called karamardikā is Carissa spina-
rum Linn.
