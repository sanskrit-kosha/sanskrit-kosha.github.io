= C. OBLIQUA WILLD. VAR. WALLICHII C. B. CLARKE; see Hooker 4, p. 137;
(4) BAUHINIA VARIEGATA LINN. (Dgv, nr. 121, = kovidāra; KB 2,
p. 898-900);
(5) B. ACUMINATA LINN. (Kav, p. 620: acc. to Gaṅgādhara).
