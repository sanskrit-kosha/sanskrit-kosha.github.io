(1) URARIA LAGOPOIDES DC. (Avk; Bpn, p. 287; Chopra; Dutt;
DWH 1, p. 426-428; Dy, p. 221-222; HK; Kav; KB 1, p. 750-751;
Nadk. 1, nr. 2542; PA 1, 2, p. 96; Sanyal; V 6, p. 109-110; Vśs) =
U. LAGOPODIOIDES MERR. = DOODIA LAGOPODIOIDES

as a synonym of U. LAGOPOIDES DC., which is incorrect; see also

JACQ.;
(4) DESMODIUM GANGETICUM DC. (PA 1, 2, p. 96-104) = D. COLLI-
NUM ROXB. = HEDYSARUM COLLINUM ROXB. = H.
GANGETICUM LINN.;
(5) DESMODIUM LATIFOLIUM DC. (PA 1, 2, p. 96: alternate source of
pṛśniparṇī) = D. COLLINUM WALL. = HEDYSARUM LATIFOLIUM ROXB.;
(6) HEMIONITIS CORDIFOLIA ROXB.; this
plant is not recorded in the Index Kewensis, but has been described
by Roxburgh (Flora Indica, p. 756).
