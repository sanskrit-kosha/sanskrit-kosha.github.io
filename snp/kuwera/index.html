kuṭhera (ka)

(1) OCIMUM BASILICUM LINN. (Avk; Bpn, p. 511-512); [Page-544+ 36]
(2) O. SANCTUM LINN. (KB 3, p. 1967; MW?);
(3) O. GRATISSIMUM LINN. (MW?);
(4) O. CANUM SIMS (KB 3, p. 1961);
(5) a) CEDRELA TOONA ROXB. EX ROTTL. (MW);
b) = nandīvṛkṣa (PW; Vśs); PW: the white variety of tulasī;
Vśs: the white variety of kṣudratulasī (see tulasī).
Ḍa. on Su.Sū. 38, 18: arjaka is called śvetakuṭheraka when it has
minute leaves and is odourless; Ḍa. on Su.Sū. 46, 221: kuṭheraka is a
variety of barbarikā; Aruṇa on A.h.Sū. 6, 106: = vaikuṇṭhaka; He
on A. h.Sū. 6, 106: = kālamālā; Dn, varga 4, 54: = arjaka = vai-
kuṇṭha; Rn, varga 10, 84-85: = arjaka = kṣudratulasī (see tulasī).
