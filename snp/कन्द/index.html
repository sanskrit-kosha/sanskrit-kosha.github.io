kanda

(1) Amorphophallus campanulatus (Roxb.) Bl. ex Decne. (Bpn, śāka-
varga 91-93; KB 4, p. 2609-2610). The modern Bengali name of this plant
is ol. Śivadāsa (a Bengali) on Cakradatta, amlapitta 26: kanda =
vanya ola; ola is acc. to V. 4, p. 66 a synonym of sūraṇakanda; vanasū-
raṇa is mentioned by Ananta 8, 656.
(2) Chionachne koenigii (Spreng.) Thw. = Coix koenigii Spreng. =
Polytoca barbata Stapf (Chopra).
