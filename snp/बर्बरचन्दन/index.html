barbaracandana

Dn, varga 3, 9: barbarika is a white, odourless type of candana;
Rn, varga, 12, 17: barbaraka, (also called) white barbaraka, is (a kind of
candana that is) fragrant; a synonym (see Dn and Rn) is barbarottha,
explained in PW as white sandalwood.
