(1) SETARIA ITALICA BEAUV. (Avk; Chopra; Dutt; DWH 3, p. 619;
HK; KB 4, p. 2718-2719; Nadk. 1, nr. 2291; V 2, p. 113; Watt CP, p.
988-989) = PANICUM ITALICUM LINN. (Ainslie 1, p. 226-227; Avk;
Chopra; Dutt; Gul; HK; Nadk. 1, nr. 1793; PW; Roxb., p. 101-102;
Vśs; Watt CP, p. 988-989);
(2) PANICUM FRUMENTACEUM (Nadk. 1, nr. 1791); Nadk. considers
P. FRUM. as a synonym of SETARIA ITALICA BEAUV., but this is not
confirmed by the Index Kewensis, which distinguishes two species:
P. COLONUM LINN. = P. FRUMENTACEUM ROXB., and SORHGUM VUL-
GARE PERS. = P. FRUMENTACEUM SALISB.; Hooker (7, p. 30-32) re-
gards P. FRUMENTACEUM ROXB. as a synonym of P. CRUS-GALLI LINN.
VAR. FRUMENTACEUM and describes P. COLONUM LINN. as a separate
species; Cooke (3, p. 446-447) says that P. FRUMENTACEUM ROXB. is
a synonym of P. STAGNINUM RETZ. VAR. FRUMENTACEA TRIM.;
(3) PAPAVER DUBIUM (Bpn, p. 653); the Index Kewensis mentions
two plants of this name: P. DUBIUM LINN., and P. DUBIUM TENORE =
P. PINNATIFIDUM MORIS.; it may be that P. D. LINN. is meant, because
this species is also described in the Flora Iranica.
