gokṣura (ka)

(1) TRIBULUS TERRESTRIS LINN. (Avk; Bpn, p. 292-293; Chopra;
Dgv, nr. 60; Dutt; DWH 1, p. 243-245; Dy, p. 119-120; Ghosh; Gul;
HB; HK; Kav; KB 1, p. 420-423; Na 1, nr. 83; Nadk. 1, nr. 2496;
PA 1, 2, p. 75-79; Sanyal; V 3, p. 128) = T. LANUGINOSUS LINN.
(Dutt; HK; Kav; MW; Nadk. 1, nr. 2496) = T. ZEYLANICUS;
(2) T. ALATUS DELILE (Bpn, p. 292-293: may be used as a substitute);
(3) PEDALIUM MUREX LINN. (Bpn, p. 292-293: bṛhadgokṣura; KB 3,
p. 1856-1857; PA 1, 2, p. 75, footnote: considered by some as a large
form of gokṣura);
(4) HYGROPHILA SPINOSA T. ANDERS. = ASTERACANTHA LONGIFOLIA
NEES (MW; PW);
(5) BARLERIA LONGIFLORA LINN. F. (KB 3, p. 1882).
