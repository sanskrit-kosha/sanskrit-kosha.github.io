karkāru (ka)

(1) = vaḍava trapuṣa (or trapusa) (Ḍa. on Su. Sū. 46, 216 and Ci. 31, 5);
(2) = kūṣmāṇḍa (Ḍa. on Su. Ci. 18, 29);
(3) = kūṣmāṇḍabheda (Śivadāsa on Cakradatta, mūtrakṛcchra 26; Kusu-
māvalī on Vṛnda’s Siddhayoga 41, 6);
(4) = karkaṭībheda (Ḍa. on Su. Sū. 9, 4);
(5) = tiktatumbī (Kusumāvalī on Vṛnda’s Siddhayoga 41, 6);
(6) karkāru(ka) is identified as
a) Cucurbita pepo Linn. (DGV 5, p. 104-105; Kai.Nigh., oṣadhivarga
533-534; KB 2, p. 1156-1158);
b) Cucumis sativus Linn. (DGV 5, p. 104-105);
c) Benincasa hispida (Thunb.) Cogn. (KB 2, p. 1127-1128).
