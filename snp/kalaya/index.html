(1) PISUM SATIVUM LINN. (Bpn, p. 648; Dutt; HK; Kav; KB 1,
p. 772-773; V 8, p. 12; Vśs);
(2) P. ARVENSE LINN. (Nadk. 1, nr. 1939);
(3) LATHYRUS SATIVUS LINN. (Gul; Na 1, nr. 139). MW: a) a kind
of pea or pulse, b) a kind of plant with dark-coloured flowers; PW:
a) a kind of pea, b) another plant; Vśs: name of various leguminous
seeds; Bpn, dhānyavarga 57-58: a) kalāya = vartula = satīna =
hareṇuka: PISUM SATIVUM LINN. b) kalāya = tripuṭa = khaṇḍika:
LATHYRUS SATIVUS LINN.; Cakra on Ca. Sū. 27, 28: a) kalāya = vartu-
lakalāya = hareṇu: PISUM SATIVUM LINN. b) kalāya = tripuṭakalāya =
khaṇḍikā: LATHYRUS SATIVUS LINN.
