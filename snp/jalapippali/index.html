jalapippalī

(1) LIPPIA NODIFLORA MICHX. (Avk; Bpn, p. 470; KB 3, p. 1916-
1917); [Flora Iranica: PHYLA NODIFLORA (LINN.) GREENE = VERBENA
NODIFLORA LINN. = LIPPIA NODIFLORA (LINN.) RICH. IN MICHX.];
(2) COMMELINA SALICIFOLIA ROXB. (KB 4, p. 2536-2537); it may be
that toyapippalī is not a synonym of jalapippalī, but a different plant:
toyapippalī: SAPIUM SEBIFERUM ROXB. (Chopra; KB 3, p. 2284; Nadk.
1, nr. 2222); it is not clear whether jalapippalīgaṇḍīra (see gaṇḍīra)
is identical with jalapippalī.
