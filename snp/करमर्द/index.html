karamarda(ka)

(1) CARISSA CARANDAS LINN. Intern. Code = C. CONGESTA WIGHT (Dutt; HK; Kav); [Flora Pavagadh, p.
136: C. CONGESTA WIGHT = C. CARANDAS GRAHAM (pro parte) (NON
LINN.) = C. CARANDAS VAR. CONGESTA BEDD.]; see also Hooker 3, p.
630-631;
(2) C. DIFFUSA (Avk; Nadk. 1, nr. 450); the Index Kewensis men-
tions only C. DIFFUSA RIDLEY, a species from the Malayan peninsula,
but Hooker describes vol. 3, p. 631 a species from India: C. SPINARUM
DC. = C. DIFFUSA ROXB.; see Roxb., p. 231-232: C. DIFFUSA ROXB.,
karamadikā;
(3) CAPPARIS CARANDAS BURM. F. (Avk; Nadk. 1, nr. 449 and 477);
(4) FLACOURTIA CATAPHRACTA ROXB. EX WILLD. (Vśs: = pānīyā-
malaka) [= F. JANGOMAS (LOUR.) RAEUSCH.] [Chopra] = F. JANGOMAS
RAEUSCH. = F. JANGOMAS STEUD. = STIGMAROTA JANGOMAS LOUR.
