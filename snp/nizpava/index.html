niṣpāva(ka)

(1) VIGNA SINENSIS SAVI EX HASSK. = DOLICHOS SINENSIS LINN.
(Dutt; HK; Kav; MW; Nadk. 1, nr. 867; PW) = D. CYLINDRICUS
MOENCH. (Nadk. 1, nr. 867); Dutt and HK consider, in agreement with
Hooker (2, p. 205-206), D. SINENSIS LINN. as a synonym of VIGNA
CATIANG ENDL.; the latter is not recorded in the Index Kewensis; see
about the nomenclature Purseglove 1, p. 322;
(2) VIGNA CATJANG WALP. (KB 1, p. 800-802; Vśs: = rājamāṣa);
see Cooke 1, p. 404-405; Duthie 1, p. 209 and Purseglove 1, p. 322;
(3) DOLICHOS LABLAB LINN. (Gul; KB 1, p. 806-807; Na 1, nr. 151;
V 7, p. 100) = LABLAB VULGARIS SAVI (Bpn, p. 646); see Watt CP,
p. 508-510;
(4) PHASEOLUS MUNGO LINN. = PH. RADIATUS LINN. (Avk: śvetaniṣ-
pāva; Vśs).
