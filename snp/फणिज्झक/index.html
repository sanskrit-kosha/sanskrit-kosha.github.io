phaṇijjhaka = phaṇijja(ka)

(1) a) MAJORANA HORTENSIS MOENCH. [Intern. Code] = ORIGANUM
MAJORANA LINN. (Bpn, p. 510; HB; KB 3, p. 1985-1986; MW;
PW; V 8, p. 39);
b) Dn, varga 4, 52: = marubaka = kharapatra = jambīra;
Rn, varga 10, 80: = marubaka = kharapatra = gandhapatra;
Ḍa. on Su. Sū. 38, 18: = marubaka = tīkṣṇagandha;
c) = madhurajambīra (see jambīra) (Vśs);
(2) a) a species of Ocimum (Kav);
b) a kind of tulasī with small leaves (Vśs);
c) fragrant tulasī (Vśs);
(3) POGOSTEMON PARVIFLORUS BENTH. (V 6, p. 41);
(4) PIPER NIGRUM LINN. (HK: = marica, but marica is also a name
of MAJORANA HORTENSIS MOENCH.);
(5) = palāśa (Vśs).
