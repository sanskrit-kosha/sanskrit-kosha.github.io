b) S. THEAEFOLIA D. DON;

42: paṭṭikālodhra, valkalodhra) = S. PANICULATA WALL. EX D. DON

(4) S. SPICATA ROXB. (PA 1, 3, p. 65-76);
(5) DESMODIUM PULCHELLUM BENTH. (PA 1, 3, p. 75-76) = HEDY-
SARUM PULCHELLUM LINN. (PA 1, 3, p. 75) = DICERMA PULCHELLUM
DC. (PA 1, 3, p. 75, footnote: “This plant has been equated as lodhra
by Rama Rao in his ‘Flowering plants of Travancore’ and Sir R. N.
Chopra in his ‘Indigenous drugs of India’; it is, however, very doubtful
whether this plant is used as the source of lodhra any where in Travan-
core-Cochin or in India”).
